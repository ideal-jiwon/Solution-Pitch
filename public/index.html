About US

At SolutionPitch, we believe that local businesses are the heart of every community. That’s why we created this platform — to empower small and medium-sized businesses by helping them understand what their customers truly think, and how they can improve. Our app analyzes online reviews, compares them with nearby competitors, and generates actionable insights to help shop owners grow smarter and faster. Whether you run a cozy café, a buzzing bar, or a neighborhood boutique, SolutionPitch gives you the tools to stand out in your local market.
